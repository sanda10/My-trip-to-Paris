My trip to Paris

Paris, capital of France, is one of the most important and influential cities in the world. In terms of tourism, Paris is the second most visited city in Europe after London. Some of the most memorable things to do in Paris include visiting the Eiffel Tower, the Arc de Triomphe, and Notre Dame Cathedral.
